Frequently asked questions

How do we pay?

Most guests pay cash on the day in GBP or EUR. Card or bank transfer in advance may be possible ‚Äì please ask when you book.

Is the tour suitable for children?

Yes. We regularly host families with young children. We simply ask that children follow instructions and stay close to adults near the macaques.

What should we wear?

Comfortable shoes, a light jacket (it can be windy at the top) and sunscreen in warmer months. Avoid loose plastic bags around the monkeys.

Can you work around cruise or flight times?

In most cases, yes. Tell us your arrival and departure times when you enquire so we can suggest the best slot.
